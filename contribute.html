Help teach the worm tracker

This lab streams a live planarian (a flatworm) and tracks where it is and what it's doing. The tracker learns from frames a human has marked. You can add a few — it takes seconds and needs no special knowledge.

Your task: click directly on the worm in each image — it's the small dark shape on the light dish, then press Enter (or click Confirm). If you genuinely can't see it, press n for No worm visible. Unsure? Press s to Skip.
