Activity & rhythms

Activity by time of day (clock hour)

The rig is under constant room light, so this is descriptive clock-hour activity — any rhythm reflects the worm's own cycle or lab disturbance, not a light schedule. The shaded band is the 95% confidence interval across days; with only a few days it's wide, and because the activity bursts land at different clock times on different days it can't yet claim a consistent daily rhythm. It tightens as more days accumulate.
